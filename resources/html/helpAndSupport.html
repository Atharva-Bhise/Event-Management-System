Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

To create an event, go to the dashboard and click on the "Create Event" button. Fill in the required details and click "Save".

You can manage attendees by selecting the event. And then you can add, edit, or remove attendees.

If you experience any technical issues, please contact us via the "Contact" button available on the Home Screen.

To book an event, navigate to the "Events" section, choose the event you are interested in, and click the "Book Now" button. Follow the prompts to complete your booking.

To host an event, you need to have an account. Ensure you have all the event details ready, including date, time, location, and capacity. Go to "Create Event" to get started.
